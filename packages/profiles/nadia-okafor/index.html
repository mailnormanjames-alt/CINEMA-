About Nadia

From Thread to Screen

Nadia Okafor is a Costume Designer whose work bridges the precision of period craftsmanship with the boldness of contemporary fashion. She grew up in Lagos watching her mother transform Ankara fabric into architectural masterpieces — each fold a calculation, each seam a bridge between worlds. After studying at the Royal College of Art, she brought that global perspective to the art of dressing characters.

Her approach is rooted in deep research and material intimacy. Before sketching a single silhouette, Nadia spends weeks in fabric libraries, touching textiles, understanding their weight, their movement, their history. Every costume is built from the fiber up.

Her work has been recognized by the British Film Institute, the Costume Designers Guild, and威尼斯 Film Festival. She believes that costume design is not decoration — it is storytelling in its most tactile form.
